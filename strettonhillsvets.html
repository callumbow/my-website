About Stretton Hills Vets

Located at dudgeley Farm in the picturesque Shropshire countryside, Stretton Hills Vets has been providing expert equine veterinary care for over 20 years.

Our practice is dedicated exclusively to horses, offering comprehensive veterinary services from routine health checks to emergency care.

We understand the special bond between horse and owner, and we're committed to providing compassionate, professional care tailored to your horse's individual needs.

Our Services

Routine Health Checks

Comprehensive wellness exams, vaccinations, dental care, and parasite control to keep your horse in peak condition.

Lameness Evaluation

Advanced diagnostic techniques to identify and treat musculoskeletal issues, including digital radiography and ultrasound.

Emergency Care

24/7 emergency services with rapid response times across Shropshire and surrounding areas.

Diagnostic Services

On-site laboratory for blood work, endoscopy, and other diagnostic procedures with quick results.

Reproductive Services

Breeding soundness exams, artificial insemination, pregnancy diagnosis, and foaling assistance.

Pre-Purchase Exams

Thorough veterinary examinations to help you make informed decisions when purchasing a new horse.
